Everest Base Camp Trek

Trek Details

Destination

Nepal

Route

Kathmandu - Lukla - Phakding - Namche Bazaar - Tengboche - Dingboche - Lobuche - Gorak Shep - Everest Base Camp

Number of Days

14 days

Dates

1st April 2023 - 14th April 2023

Description

This trek is perfect for those who want to experience the beauty of the Himalayas and challenge themselves with high altitude trekking. You'll be trekking through some of the most stunning landscapes in the world, including the Khumbu Valley and the Sagarmatha National Park. Along the way, you'll also get to experience the unique Sherpa culture and visit ancient monasteries.
